At Riwak, Our Signature Is Home

From your dream room to your dream house, we help you create warm spaces for yourself and your loved ones everywhere.

Riwak first started with a group of friends who had to move away for their studies, forced to leave their homes behind. They found comfort in the pieces they carried from home, in their hearts, and in their new rooms — remindersof warmth, love, and belonging.

In Riwak, we aspire to craft timeless pieces that bring comfort and beauty to every home, creating wonderful memories that last for generations.
