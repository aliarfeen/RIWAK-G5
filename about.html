RIWAK STORY

At Riwak, our signature is Home.

From your very first room to the home of your dreams, we help you create warm spaces where memories are made and shared with those you love.

Riwak was born from the journey of friends who had to leave their homes behind to pursue their studies. Far from the familiar, they discovered comfort in the pieces they carried with them — reminders of warmth, love, and belonging.

Inspired by this experience, Riwak was founded with one mission: to craft timeless pieces that bring comfort and beauty to every home, creating memories that last for generations.

Whether you are a student decorating your first apartment, a couple starting a new chapter, an expecting parent preparing a nursery, or simply embarking on a new adventure — Riwak is here to be part of your story.
